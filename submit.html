How to submit

Vedanceity does not host PDFs. To be indexed, submissions must be archived on Zenodo and have an assigned DOI.

  • Upload your paper to Zenodo (record must be public)
  • Include complete metadata: title, authors, abstract, keywords
  • Provide the DOI link for indexing (send below)

Email submissions / DOI links to: vedanceity@gmail.com

Example DOI link format:
https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.1234567

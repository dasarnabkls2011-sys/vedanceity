Vedanceity

Open research index for theoretical and interdisciplinary work. Permanent DOI-first archiving — simple, citable, human-readable.

Overview

Vedanceity curates DOI-backed records (Zenodo), surfaces metadata, and links to authoritative archival copies. We do not host PDFs — we link to the canonical archive for citation and long-term preservation.

How it works

  • Authors upload a public record to Zenodo and obtain a DOI.
  • Send the DOI link to our email for indexing.
  • We verify metadata, then list the work with a canonical link to the archive.

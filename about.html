About Vedanceity

An independent, open-access research index with a DOI-first approach — curated for theoretical, interdisciplinary, and emerging scientific work.

Mission

To provide permanent, citable access to high-quality research using open infrastructure such as Zenodo and DOI-based preservation. We prioritize discoverability, author ownership, and transparent metadata.

How we operate

  • Index DOI-backed records (Zenodo canonical copies).
  • Verify metadata and surface authoritative links for citation.
  • Maintain a minimal, non-commercial index — we do not host PDFs.

Principles

Open Access

Free, barrier-free access for readers and authors.

DOI-first

We index canonical DOI records for persistent citation links.

Author ownership

Authors retain IP; we link to author-provided archives.

Non-commercial

Community-driven index without paywalls or ads.

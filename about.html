About Vedanceity

Vedanceity is an independent, open-access research archive dedicated to theoretical, interdisciplinary, and emerging scientific work.

Mission

To provide permanent, citable access to high-quality research using open infrastructure such as Zenodo and DOI-based preservation.

Principles

  • Open access without paywalls
  • Permanent DOI-based archiving
  • Author-owned intellectual property
  • Non-commercial and community-driven
